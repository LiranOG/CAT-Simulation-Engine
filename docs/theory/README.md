## Scope

Theory documentation describes the conceptual basis of the project. It should
remain distinct from implementation details, although it may refer to equations
used by the engine when those equations clarify the theory.

The current theory surface includes:

- Bound I: Chrono-Optical Horizon.
- Bound II: Asynchronous Gap.
- Bound III: Hive-Mind Anomaly.
- Biological constraints on intelligence.
- The "coin landing on its edge" analogy.
- Falsifiability and scientific status.

## Maintenance Rules

- Keep the prose professional, English-only, and suitable for international
  research review.
- Do not quote source notes in Hebrew; translate and synthesize instead.
- Keep speculative claims clearly labeled as speculative.
- Keep empirical claims distinguishable from philosophical interpretation.
